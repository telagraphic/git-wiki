No Stretch

You'll need to wrap h1, p, ul, ol and anything else within a section tag with a class of no-stretch. 

    

      <section class="no-stretch">
        <h2>No Stretch</h1>
        <p>
          You'll need to wrap <b>h1, p, ul, ol</b>.
        </p>
      </section>
